About Us

“Have you ever been at sea in a dense fog, when it seemed as if a tangible white darkness shut you in and the great ship, tense and anxious, groped her way toward the shore with plummet and sounding-line, and you waited with beating heart for something to happen? I was like that ship before my education began, only I was without compass or sounding line, and no way of knowing how near the harbor was. “Light! Give me light!” was the wordless cry of my soul, and the light of love shone on me in that very hour.”
-Helen Keller

High Five is an initiave by 5 college students:Mayuri Samanta, Swapnil Shrivastava, Shubham Sharma, Muskan Saxena, Rashi Srivastava, who want to help school going students in continuing education right from their home in this time of pandemic and even after. We offer several courses under various domains such as Science, Commerce, Arts.
